About AI Skincare & Makeup Scanner

Welcome to AI Skincare & Makeup Scanner, where artificial intelligence meets beauty and skincare. Our platform helps users analyze their skin type, detect skincare concerns, and choose the most suitable beauty products, including makeup and skincare items.

We aim to simplify skincare and makeup selection through AI-powered recommendations, ensuring that every product you use is perfectly suited for your skin type and tone.

Our advanced AI technology detects skin issues such as acne, pigmentation, and dark spots, offering personalized skincare recommendations to help you maintain healthy, radiant skin.

Beyond skincare, our real-time makeup try-on feature allows users to test foundation, lipstick, and concealer shades using their device's camera. Get instant feedback on what works best for your complexion.

Our Features

Advanced AI Face Analysis: Our system scans your face using AI and machine learning to detect skin types, tone, and problem areas, giving you the best skincare routine.

Personalized Skincare Recommendations: Get AI-driven suggestions for cleansers, moisturizers, and serums based on your unique skin needs.

Real-Time Virtual Makeup Try-On: Experiment with different makeup products like lipstick, foundation, and concealer to see which shades suit you best.

AI-Powered Product Matching: Browse our database to find the best cosmetics and skincare products, complete with ingredient breakdowns and suitability scores.
